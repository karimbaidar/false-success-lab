# Code of Conduct

False Success Lab contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful,
practical, and focused on helping people understand false-success risks in AI
workflows.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence.
- Keep feedback focused on code, docs, scenarios, and claims.
- Avoid sharing secrets, private customer data, or proprietary repository code.
- Label uncertain scanner findings honestly as review prompts.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Intentionally misleading claims about what the lab or scanner proves.
- Adding examples that include real credentials, regulated records, or private
  customer data.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate
this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in blocked
participation.

Report concerns privately to `karimbaidar@yahoo.com`.
